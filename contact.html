Thomas Levinson

Work

I’m an illustrator and visual journalist currently based in New York. I grew up on the sunny side of Southern California, where I first became interested in visual storytelling and drawing as a way to make sense of the world.

My approach to illustration is shaped by a background in photojournalism, developed through newsroom editorial work across the United States. That experience continues to inform my emphasis on narrative, character, and observational detail.

My work has appeared in The Daily Beast, The New York Post, and the New York Daily News.

I’m currently a photo editor at The Daily Beast, while continuing to focus on illustration for editorial, entertainment, and political work.
